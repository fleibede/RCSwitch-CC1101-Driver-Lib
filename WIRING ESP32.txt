CC1101 ESP32 wiring


GPIO	ESP32  	/	CC1101
------------------------------------
3V3	(3,3V)	/	VCC
G	(GND)	/	GND
18	(SCK)	/	SCK
19	(MISO)	/	MISO
23	(MOSI)	/	MOSI
5	(SS)	/	CSN
2	(PWM)	/	GDO0 (TX)
4	(PWM)	/	GDO2 (RX)
